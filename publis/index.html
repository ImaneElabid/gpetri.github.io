Short Bio

I graduated on Computer Science at Famaf, University of Córdoba, Argentina in August 2005. Currently I am pursuing a Ph.D. (expected for 2010) under the supervision of Gérard Boudol, at the INDES team. I also enjoyed working with Marieke Huisman at the Everest team.

Research Interests

My current research topic is memory models and semantics of shared memory parallel systems. I also like verification and formal methods in general.

Publications

  • Relaxed memory models: an operational approach, with Gérard Boudol at POPL'09.

  • BicolanoMT: a formalization of multi-threaded Java at bytecode level, with Marieke Huisman at Bytecode'08.

  • The Java memory model: a formal explanation, with Marieke Huisman in Verification and Analysis of Multi-threaded Java-like Programs (VAMP'07).
